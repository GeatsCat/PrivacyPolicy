Privacy Policy

1. Scope of Application
This Privacy Policy applies to the collection, processing, utilization, transfer, and protection of personal data when you participate in activities within our games and affiliated websites.

2. Purpose and Categories of Data Collection
Depending on the services we provide, we may collect your personal data in the following situations and categories:

(1) Game Participation
When you apply to join a game, your personal data, including your name, nickname, and other necessary information, will be collected. After becoming a member, you will receive a membership account and password, which you can use to access various membership services.
Additionally, data related to your computer hardware, software, and mobile devices, including your browser type, login time, and website addresses, may be collected. We use this data to help maintain the operation of the game and ensure service quality.

(2) Online Transactions
If you use online transaction services within the game, we will verify the order information you submit based on the type of transaction requested, ensuring that you can complete payments, recharge, and other related consumption services.

(3) Online Events
When you participate in our online events or surveys, your personal data will be directly verified through our membership database. However, if the event requires you to provide additional information, you will need to fill in the necessary data accordingly. This supplementary data is also protected under this Privacy Policy.

(4) Browsing Products and Websites
When you browse our websites or affiliated sites, our servers will automatically record your connection time and browsing history. This information is used solely to enhance and optimize our online services and improve service quality. The data is only used for aggregated analysis and is not linked to any specific individual.

(5) Others
With your consent or for the purpose of fulfilling a contract, your personal data may be provided to us. This data will only be used and processed within the agreed scope of services and will not be used for other purposes.

3. Duration, Region, Recipients, and Methods of Data Usage

(1) Duration of Use
Unless otherwise stipulated by law, your personal data will be used until the services within the game and its affiliated websites are completed or until you request to cease using the services.

(2) Region of Use
Your data will be used in regions where we and our affiliated websites provide services, as well as in the locations of other partner companies that you have agreed to under the privacy policy.

(3) Recipients of Use
Unless otherwise stipulated by law, your personal data will only be used for the agreed-upon service purposes and within our internal operations and relevant connected scopes. We will not arbitrarily transfer your personal data to third parties or use it for other purposes.

(4) Methods of Use

A. User Information
When you access game services, we may collect relevant information about your device as follows:

Information you provide to us (directly or through third parties).
When you provide information about yourself, such as when you register for the game (including your chosen nickname and information imported from connected social media accounts), we may collect details like your social media profile, device ID, and other user information. In certain regions, you may be asked to provide additional information stored locally on your device to verify your age or manage game systems.
In some regions, you may be required to provide an email address to verify parental or guardian consent if you are below the relevant minimum age. You may also need to provide information such as location data to customize gameplay or services.
Once your game account is set up, we may access your phone number or email address to verify your identity, ensuring you enter a code or password upon each login.
Where applicable in your region, you may opt to receive promotional emails related to our games. Additionally, you may provide information through customer service interactions, including contact details, submitted queries, and social media platform information. For data subject requests, we may collect information like your country/region, email address, user ID, and linked social media accounts to verify your identity and process requests.

B. Online Transactions
If you choose to use online transaction services, we will confirm the relevant order data you submit to ensure that you can complete payments, recharge, or logistics-related consumption services.

C. Online Events and Surveys
The personal data you provide when participating in our online events or surveys will be used solely for the purposes of that event or survey.

D. Statistics and Analysis
We periodically analyze and statistically process the data collected from your game account registration and browsing activities on our website. This analysis is only conducted at an aggregate level, not targeting individual behaviors.

8.Rights under CCPA (California Consumer Privacy Act)

If you are a California resident and CCPA applies to you without any recognized exceptions, you have the following rights:

Right to Know: You can request the following information covering the previous 12 months at no cost:

The categories of personal information we have collected about you.

The categories of sources from which the personal information was collected.

The purposes for which the personal information was collected.

The categories of third parties with whom we share personal information, the specific personal information categories shared, and the purpose of sharing.

Specific pieces of personal information we have collected about you.

Right to Delete: You can request that we delete the personal information we have collected from you, except where CCPA recognizes specific exceptions.

Right to Correct: You can request that we correct inaccurate personal information that we maintain about you.

Right to Non-Discrimination: You are protected from discrimination for exercising your CCPA rights. This includes not being subjected to different levels or quality of services or being denied goods or services when exercising your CCPA rights.

Our goal is to complete all verified requests within 45 days as required by CCPA. If an extension is necessary, we will provide an explanation for any delay.

Right to know whether your personal data is sold:

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

How to Exercise Your Rights

Manage Your Data: Log in to your account to manage your data directly. If you are a California resident covered by CCPA, you can also exercise your rights related to other data by contacting us. We may need to verify your identity before complying with your request to protect your privacy and security. If we cannot verify your identity, we may deny your request.

Authorized Agents: Under CCPA, you can designate an authorized agent to make requests on your behalf. To process such a request, you must provide the agent with signed written permission. We reserve the right to verify the agent’s identity and directly confirm your authorization for the request.

Right to Opt-Out of the Sale or Sharing of Personal Information: If you are a California resident:

You have the right at any time to direct businesses that sell or share your personal information to stop doing so. This right is also known as the "right to opt-out" of the sale or sharing of personal information.

Upon receiving your notification, we will inform you whether your personal information can be sold or shared, and you can opt out.

If we have actual knowledge that you are under 16, we will not sell or share your personal information without your (if you are between 13 and 16 years old) or your parent’s/guardian’s (if you are under 13) explicit consent.

If we have received your opt-out request, we are prohibited from selling or sharing your personal information without your permission.

Right to Access Information: You can request that we disclose the information we collect, use, or share and provide details about our data practices.

Right to Delete: You can request that we delete personal data collected from you unless specific legal exceptions apply.

Right to Correct: You can request that we correct inaccurate personal data we maintain about you.
